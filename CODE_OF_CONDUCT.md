# Code of Conduct

`claudex` should be a useful, low-drama place to collaborate.

## Standards

Please be respectful, specific, and constructive. Good participation includes:

- explaining tradeoffs clearly
- assuming good faith
- keeping criticism focused on the work
- respecting privacy around local transcripts and development data
- helping newcomers understand the project shape

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, personal attacks, deliberate intimidation, sustained disruption, or sharing someone else's private information.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and issues that make the project hostile or unsafe. Repeated or severe violations may lead to being blocked from participating.

If no private maintainer contact is listed yet, open an issue asking for a private contact channel without including sensitive details.
